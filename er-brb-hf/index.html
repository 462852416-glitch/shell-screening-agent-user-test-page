Hard Filters as evidence gating — not final ranking
这个页面只展示三层：Universe Construction、Hard Filters、Deep Due Diligence。 ER/BRB 只用于回答一个问题：这家公司能不能进入 Deep DD，而不是用于最终商业排序。核心原则写死在页面里：未发现风险证据 ≠ 风险不存在。
Design intent. Universe 只负责高召回构建候选池，不做 ER/BRB 判断；User Appetite Setting 进入 HF 前作为控制面板；HF-1 到 HF-7 输出 belief distribution；整体结论按业务优先级聚合，不允许低估值和平台故事掩盖 HF-4 / HF-5 / HF-6 的硬伤。

Step 2 · Hard Filters HF-1 to HF-7
ER/BRB-style belief distribution for DD entry
每个 HF 输出四类 belief：Pass / Needs DD / Exclude / Insufficient Evidence。整体结论按业务优先级而不是简单平均来聚合。
HF-6 veto aware Unknown preserved
Critical principle. The most dangerous failure mode is not “finding risk”; it is pretending incomplete evidence means clean risk. So this layer is designed to preserve uncertainty explicitly, especially in HF-3, HF-4, and HF-5.
